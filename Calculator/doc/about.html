About the program

The author of this program is Prof. Carl Burch, who you can contact at cburch@cburch.com. It was begun in Spring 2003 for students at College of St Benedict and St John's University in Collegeville, Minnesota.

If you find this program useful, the author would love to hear about it: My name is Carl Burch, at cburch@cburch.com. I will appreciate hearing from teachers at other institutions who find it useful in their classes.

If you find any bugs, please let me know! I generally respond to bug reports fairly quickly. If you think that a feature is missing that would help the program immensely, I'm also curious.
